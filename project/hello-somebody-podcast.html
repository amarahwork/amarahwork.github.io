Challenge

Hello Somebody was a podcast hosted by Nina Turner in which she discussed current social and political events through her unique perspective. To introduce a new season of the Hello Somebody podcast, the client wanted a refresh of existing branding. The client wanted to keep the same color palette and use of the glasses motif, but requested new type treatment and a more modular design for the promotional graphics.

Solution

The bright palette was used in gentle gradients to soften the overal appearance of the graphics. A more feminine type family was chosen, and Nina Turner's iconic glasses were redrawn to match the receding thin lines of the type. A glow motif was introduced to unify these elements with the waveform used in the episode preview graphics. Additionally, alternative designs were created to highlight special episodes of the podcast.
